Bacon Burger (USA Style)

We're going all-out born in the USA for this one

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Bacon
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy and the beef patty until browned and cooked through.
  2. Toast the burger buns and layer with lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, bacon, and the cooked patty.
  3. Add your preferred sauce, assemble the burger, and serve.
