Spaghetti Bolognese (Nana's Style)

Nana cooked up a storm on this one. Get ready to tuck in!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Tomato Passata
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine (Optional)
  • Beef stock
  • Bay leaf
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the onion, garlic, carrot, and celery in olive oil until softened, then add the minced beef and brown it.
  2. Stir in tomato paste, passata, chopped tomatoes, red wine (if using), beef stock, and herbs; season and simmer gently until thickened.
  3. Boil the spaghetti until al dente, drain, then serve topped with the sauce and Parmesan cheese.
