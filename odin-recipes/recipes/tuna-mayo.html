Tuna Mayo (Summer Style)

It's a heatwave in the UK, and that means healthy cold food!

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Drain the tuna and place it in a bowl.
  2. Mix in mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  3. Serve on bread, crackers, or as a salad.
